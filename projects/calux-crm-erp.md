# Calux CRM + ERP — Mexican Lighting Manufacturer

**Client:** Calux Iluminacion, Mexico &nbsp;·&nbsp; **Year:** 2024

## Overview
Two-system solution for a Mexican lighting manufacturer: a Perfex-based CRM for client and sales management, and a custom ERP with a Ramos delivery logistics module.

## Systems
- **calux-crm** — Perfex CRM customized for Calux's sales pipeline and client management
- **calux-erp** — Custom ERP with inventory, invoicing, and the Ramos delivery logistics module

## My Role
- Extended Perfex CRM with custom modules for the Mexican market (Spanish localization, MXN currency)
- Built the ERP delivery module: route planning, driver assignment, delivery confirmation
- Integrated both systems via shared client/order data

## Tech Stack
Perfex CRM · PHP · Laravel · MySQL · JavaScript

## Key Decisions
- Used Perfex as the CRM base to reduce development time while building the ERP from scratch for full control over logistics workflows
